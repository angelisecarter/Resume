Favorite Consoles and Games

Favorite Consoles

I have many gaming consoles, maybe too many. But they all seem to have different purposes. Here's my list of the consoles I still use, and the games I like.

  1. Playstation 5
    Best for solo gaming.
    Play with others online but when I'm the only one playing at home and I want to get serious gaming with superior graphics, this is it!

  2. Meta Quest 2
    Best VR experience.
    I use this more for puzzle games and working out. The fitness games can you give you a great workout. Beat Saber anyone?

  3. Nintendo Switch
    Best for gaming with kids.
    Lots of great games for kids once they get the hang of using a controller. You can even get an augmented reality race car to play along.

  4. XBox 360
    Best hands-free experience
    I still use this console mainly for dancing games. It's nice to not have to hold any controllers. The newer Xbox is definitely a rival to PlayStation but I prefer the PS games ecosystem.

  5. Wii
    Best for gaming with young kids or adults that don't game
    Kids that are too young to work a controller will find this super easy to use. It's an older console so the graphic so don't expect up-to-date graphics.

Favorite Games

I have many gaming consoles, maybe too many. But they all seem to have different purposes. Here's my list of the consoles I still use, and the games I like.

  1. Playstation 5: Marvel Avengers
    Not hardcore gaming but what I'm playing now. It's fun and you can set the difficulty, which is always nice.
    Lots of side missions too so you can get your money's worth. I always love playing as a superhero.

  2. Meta Quest 2: The Room
    If you like escape rooms and solving puzzles then this is for you!
    The graphics on this game are fantastic. And it's challenging but not to the point of frustration. My only complaint is that it was too short.

  3. Nintendo Switch: KeyWe
    I wasn't sure about this when I bought it but it has been a lot of fun with my kid.
    In co-op mode, you are two little birds trying to beat the clock and finish your tasks. Great for learing to work together.

  4. XBox 360: Michael Jackson Experience
    Still the best dance game for me.
    The music is awesome of course and it's a lot of fun trying to do his moves.

  5. Wii: Sports Resort
    No previous experience with gaming needed to play and a great variety of games to compete with your friends.
    Test your skills with Wakeboarding, Frisbee, Archery, and more. It's all about the technique.
